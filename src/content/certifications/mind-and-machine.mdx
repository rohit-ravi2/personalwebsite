Four-course specialization from CU Boulder's Institute for Cognitive Science on the intersection of human cognition and artificial intelligence — what the "mind" is, how problems get solved (biologically and computationally), computational vision, and the developmental/evolutionary context of cognition.

**Courses**
1. What is "the mind" and what is artificial intelligence?
2. Methods for Solving Problems
3. Computational Vision
4. Interpersonal, Developmental, and Evolutionary Perspectives of the Mind
