MOOC from the University of Washington, taught by Rajesh P. N. Rao (CSE) and Adrienne Fairhall (Physiology & Biophysics). Covers neural coding, computational models of single-neuron and network dynamics, and learning rules — the foundational toolkit for reasoning quantitatively about how nervous systems compute.

Directly relevant to my work on *C. elegans* connectomics and the wiring-vs-expression question in small nervous systems.
