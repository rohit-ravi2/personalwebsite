Kaggle competition entry predicting 3D structure of RNA molecules from primary sequence, scored structurally via TM-align against experimentally determined targets.

Built a five-stage pipeline exploring base-pairing priors, sequence embeddings, and pairwise-distance featurizations; compiled a local TM-align evaluator to check structural similarity offline before submitting. Writeup: [Stanford RNA 3D Folding project page](/projects/rnafold-v2).
